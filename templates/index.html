Guide: How to Use the Storytelling Chatbot

Welcome to the Storytelling Chatbot! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use it:

  1. Type your message or query in the input field labeled "Chat to storytelling chatbot here...".
  2. Press the "Send" button to submit your message to the chatbot.
  3. Wait for the chatbot's response. It will provide you with a story or answer based on your input.
  4. If you want to exit the chat at any time, simply type "quit" and press send. The chatbot will bid you farewell.
  5. If the chatbot provides a partial story and prompts you to continue, type "continue" and press send. The chatbot will generate the next part of the story.
  6. To guide the story, use phrases like "tell me a story about [topic]" or "story about [topic]". The chatbot will ask for further details to tailor the story.
  7. Customized Stories: When you provide both a topic, a character name and kind of story, the chatbot creates a story that incorporates both. For example, typing "dog,Jason,romantic " will result will romantic story about dog(Jason).
  8. Also the chatbot can do others which provide the topic and kind of story or just the topic name
  9. Follow any additional instructions provided by the chatbot during the conversation, such as providing input for specific prompts or questions.
  10. Enjoy the storytelling experience with the chatbot!
